### C3 本体構造

```

  = Σ_O Σ_(v ∈ fiber O) onc v             -- sum_fiberwise_of_maps_to

11 * count = 11 * Σ_O onc(rep_O) = Σ_v onc v = 110n
⟹ count = 10n (Nat.eq_of_mul_eq_mul_left).
⟹ (count : F_11) = (10n : F_11).
```
