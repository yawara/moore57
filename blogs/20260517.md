### orbital trace identity の数学的 outline (docstring に追加済み)

```
Step 1: trace(A · P_int) = 110n over ℤ (Σ T_k for k=0..10)
Step 2: P_int(u,v) = 11 if u=v=fixed, 1 if u,v same free orbit, else 0
Step 3: trace(A · P_int) = Σ_v free |N(v) ∩ orbit(v)| over ℤ
Step 4: σ-invariance: 11 · |N(v_O) ∩ O| = Σ_v∈O |N(v) ∩ O|
Step 5: 軌道基底 trace = Σ_O |N(v_O) ∩ O|
Step 6: = (1/11) · 110n = 10n over ℤ. Mod 11: 10n mod 11.
```
