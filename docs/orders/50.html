Status & outlook

Not yet formalized. By Macaj–Siran Theorem 5, a Sylow-5 subgroup of G of order 25 is one of two abelian types (ℤ₂₅ or ℤ₅ × ℤ₅), each with a fixed point set isomorphic to a sub-structure of the Hoffman–Singleton graph; the involution then has a₀ = 56 and a₁ = 112 (Lemma 2). Combining these would close all five cases.
