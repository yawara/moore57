From the paper (Macaj–Siran 2010)

Theorem 7 lists 50 as a surviving even-order maximum (p = 5). Lemma 13 (p² = 25) gives detailed constraints on order-25 elements; Lemma 2 fixes involution parameters. Theorem 5 limits the Sylow-5 subgroup to two abelian types.

The paper does not exclude group order 50.
