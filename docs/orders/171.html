Status & outlook

Not yet formalized. The p = 19 Sylow part is exactly the setting of the unconditional no_D19_acts_on_Moore57 theorem when paired with an involution; in the odd case here there is no involution, so the obstruction must come from the structure on the fixed points of the order-19 element instead.

Concretely: by Lemma 12 (p = 19, a₀(x) = 1), an order-19 automorphism fixes a single vertex, so the ℤ₉- or ℤ₃²-factor acts on the star of size 56 around that fixed point. Bounding the automorphism group of that star yields the obstruction.
