Status & outlook

Not yet formalized. Macaj–Siran Theorem 4 gives the upper bound |3-part of G| ≤ 27, and Lemma 2 fixes the involution parameters (a₀ = 56, a₁ = 112); the remaining work is to rule out each of the 15 specific extensions. The Sylow-2 element is always the same single involution, so most of the proof effort lies on the 3-side via Corollary 2 (SmallGroup(81, 9)) and the triangle-free obstruction.
