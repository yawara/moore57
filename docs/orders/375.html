Source

Macaj–Siran 2010, Theorem 6 (odd case). The largest possible |G| overall (Corollary 3: |G| ≤ 375).

Group classification

There are 4 groups of order 375 (SmallGroup(375, 1) … SmallGroup(375, 4)). By Theorem 5, the Sylow-5 subgroup P of size 125 is one of two abelian types (ℤ₁₂₅ or ℤ₂₅ × ℤ₅) once SmallGroup(625, 12) is excluded (Proposition 4).

SmallGroup IDs SmallGroup(375, k) enumerate the precise extensions; we list them by family here.

Status & outlook

Not yet formalized. This is the most computationally involved candidate: the paper notes that even after Lemma 5's equitable-partition check, hundreds of candidate adjacency matrices for groups of order 375 with 10 orbits survive. The Lean route would mirror the GAP-aided Proposition 4 / 5 arguments and is correspondingly large.
